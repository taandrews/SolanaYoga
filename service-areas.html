Where I Teach

Personalized practice in the neighborhoods you love

Yoga in Your Neighborhood

I bring fully equipped, personalized yoga sessions to you—whether that's your home, office, rooftop terrace, or a beautiful outdoor space in your neighborhood. No commute, no crowded studios, just authentic practice in a space where you're comfortable.

Each Miami neighborhood has its own unique energy and character. I've had the privilege of teaching across these diverse communities, and I love how the local vibe becomes part of the practice itself.

Brickell

"Urban Energy Meets Mindful Practice"

Miami's financial district pulses with energy, ambition, and high-rise living. Brickell residents know how to hustle—and increasingly, they understand the importance of balance. I work with professionals, entrepreneurs, and urban dwellers who need yoga to counterbalance their dynamic lives.

Popular Session Locations

  • High-rise condo living rooms with skyline views
  • Rooftop terraces and sky lounges
  • Waterfront balconies overlooking Biscayne Bay
  • Private amenity rooms in luxury buildings
  • Office spaces for lunchtime sessions

Neighborhood Vibe

The practice here often focuses on stress relief, posture correction (especially for desk workers), and creating pockets of calm amidst the urban intensity. Early morning sessions before the workday and evening wind-down practices are especially popular.

Best Times

Early morning (6-8am), lunch breaks (12-1pm), after work (6-8pm)

Ideal For

Busy professionals, corporate wellness, stress management

Notable Spots

Brickell Key, Icon Brickell, Brickell Heights, SLS Brickell

Book in Brickell

Downtown Miami

"Where the City Breathes"

Downtown Miami blends business with culture, offering everything from corporate offices to bayfront parks. The energy here is cosmopolitan and fast-paced, making yoga practice an essential reset for busy urbanites.

Popular Session Locations

  • Corporate office conference rooms and wellness spaces
  • Residential high-rise condos and apartments
  • Bayfront Park for outdoor sunrise sessions
  • Museum Park green spaces
  • Private rooftop gardens

Neighborhood Vibe

Downtown clients often seek efficiency and effectiveness—yoga that fits seamlessly into their schedule while delivering maximum benefit. Lunchtime express sessions and targeted practices (like "yoga for better posture" or "stress relief in 30 minutes") are popular here.

Best Times

Sunrise outdoor sessions (6:30-7:30am), lunch breaks, early evening

Ideal For

Corporate groups, quick lunchtime sessions, outdoor enthusiasts

Notable Spots

Bayfront Park, Museum Park, Flagler Street area

Book Downtown

Midtown

"Creative Spirit, Grounded Practice"

Midtown Miami embodies artistic energy and modern urban living. This walkable neighborhood attracts creatives, young professionals, and wellness-minded residents who appreciate both culture and convenience.

Popular Session Locations

  • Modern loft apartments with natural light
  • Margaret Pace Park waterfront sessions
  • Rooftop terraces with Design District views
  • Private outdoor courtyards
  • Community wellness spaces

Neighborhood Vibe

The Midtown community embraces wellness as part of lifestyle. Practices here often incorporate creative elements—longer holds for meditation, flows synchronized to curated playlists, and outdoor sessions that take advantage of the neighborhood's green spaces and waterfront access.

Best Times

Sunrise at Margaret Pace Park, weekend mornings, sunset sessions

Ideal For

Creatives, small group sessions, outdoor practice, friend groups

Notable Spots

Margaret Pace Park, Midtown Miami complex, Wynwood border

Book in Midtown

Coconut Grove

"Bohemian Soul, Natural Connection"

Miami's oldest continuously inhabited neighborhood, Coconut Grove exudes laid-back bohemian charm. With its tree-lined streets, waterfront location, and artistic community, it's a naturally yogic environment.

Popular Session Locations

  • Private gardens and lush outdoor spaces
  • Bayfront homes with water views
  • Shaded patios under tropical canopy
  • Kennedy Park waterfront sessions
  • Historic homes with character and charm

Neighborhood Vibe

The Grove's natural beauty and relaxed atmosphere create the perfect setting for restorative practices, nature-connected flows, and meditation. Many clients here appreciate longer, more contemplative sessions that honor the neighborhood's peaceful energy.

Best Times

Early morning outdoor, weekend afternoons, golden hour sessions

Ideal For

Nature lovers, families, restorative practice, meditation focus

Notable Spots

Kennedy Park, The Barnacle, waterfront estates, tree-lined streets

Book in the Grove

Edgewater

"Waterfront Serenity, Urban Convenience"

Nestled between Downtown and the Design District, Edgewater offers stunning Biscayne Bay views and a more residential feel than its urban neighbors. It's where Miami's professionals come to live well and find balance.

Popular Session Locations

  • Waterfront condo balconies with bay breezes
  • Margaret Pace Park (shared with Midtown)
  • Rooftop pools and wellness decks
  • Private terraces overlooking the water
  • Modern living spaces with floor-to-ceiling windows

Neighborhood Vibe

Edgewater attracts residents seeking urban amenities with a calmer atmosphere. Practices here often emphasize balance—energizing morning flows before work, restorative evening sessions, and weekend practices that make the most of waterfront views and fresh air.

Best Times

Sunrise waterfront sessions, early evening, weekend mornings

Ideal For

Professionals seeking balance, outdoor enthusiasts, couples

Notable Spots

Biscayne Bay waterfront, Paraiso District, Aria Reserve

Book in Edgewater

Beyond These Neighborhoods?

While Brickell, Downtown, Midtown, Coconut Grove, and Edgewater are my primary service areas, I'm always open to traveling to nearby neighborhoods for the right fit. If you're in Coral Gables, Wynwood, the Upper East Side, or another Miami area, reach out—we can likely make it work!

Flexible Service Area

I'm happy to discuss sessions in adjacent neighborhoods. The most important thing is finding a space where you feel comfortable and can practice consistently.
