About

Yoga That Adapts to You

I'm the founder of Solana Yoga, and I believe yoga should meet you where you are—not the other way around. With over a decade of practice and teaching experience, I bring personalized yoga instruction directly to your space across Miami's most vibrant neighborhoods.

Whether you're seeking stress relief, increased flexibility, strength building, or simply a moment of peace in your busy day, I'll create a practice tailored specifically to your goals, your body, and your lifestyle.

My approach blends traditional yoga philosophy with modern wellness practices, creating sessions that are both grounding and accessible. Every body is different, every practice is personal, and every breath is an opportunity to reconnect with yourself.
