About Rachel

A Journey to Mindful Movement

Bringing authentic, personalized yoga practice to Miami

My Story

My relationship with yoga began not on a pristine studio floor, but in a small apartment during one of the most challenging periods of my life. I was working in corporate finance, burning out, and searching for something—anything—that would help me reconnect with myself.

That first yoga class was humbling. I couldn't touch my toes, my mind wouldn't quiet, and I felt completely out of place among the seemingly effortless practitioners around me. But something about the practice called me back. Not the Instagram-perfect poses or the promise of physical transformation, but the simple invitation to breathe, to be present, to accept where I was.

Over the next several years, yoga became my anchor. It taught me patience with my body, compassion for my limitations, and respect for my own journey. Eventually, I left my corporate career to pursue yoga teacher training, studying under incredible teachers in Bali, India, and throughout the United States.

When I moved to Miami five years ago, I fell in love with the city's energy—the vibrant diversity, the connection to nature, the waterfront sunrises that make everything feel possible. But I also noticed something: many people felt intimidated by traditional yoga studios, constrained by class schedules, or simply preferred the comfort and convenience of their own space.

That's why I created Yoga x Rachel McCorry. I wanted to make personalized, authentic yoga practice accessible to everyone—in your home, at your pace, designed around your unique body and goals. Whether you're a complete beginner, recovering from an injury, training for athletic performance, or simply seeking more peace in your daily life, I'm here to meet you exactly where you are.

My teaching style blends traditional yoga philosophy with modern functional movement principles. I believe yoga should feel good in your body, not just look good in a mirror. Every session I teach is customized to your needs, your goals, and your life. Because yoga isn't about perfecting a pose—it's about cultivating awareness, building strength, finding balance, and creating space to simply be.

My Approach

Teaching Philosophy

01

Yoga is for Every Body

There's no such thing as being "bad at yoga" or "not flexible enough." Your practice is uniquely yours, and I'll help you honor your body's wisdom rather than force it into predetermined shapes.

02

Function Over Form

I prioritize how a pose feels in your body over how it looks. We'll work together to find alignment that serves you, using props and modifications to make every practice accessible and effective.

03

Breath is the Foundation

Before fancy poses, we breathe. The breath is our anchor, our guide, and our greatest tool for calming the nervous system and connecting mind to body.

04

Practice, Not Perfection

Yoga is called a practice for a reason. There's no destination, no "getting it right." Every time you step on your mat is an opportunity to learn something new about yourself.

05

Your Space, Your Sanctuary

I believe bringing yoga to your space creates a more authentic, sustainable practice. No commute, no judgment, no comparison—just you, your mat, and the invitation to be present.

06

Whole-Person Wellness

Yoga extends far beyond the physical. I incorporate breathwork, meditation, and mindfulness techniques to support your mental, emotional, and physical wellbeing.

My Home

Why Miami?

Miami's energy is unmatched. The way the city embraces both hustle and relaxation, the connection to water and nature, the cultural diversity that brings so many perspectives together—this city embodies the balance yoga teaches us to find.

I love teaching in Miami because every neighborhood has its own personality. From the sleek high-rises of Brickell to the bohemian charm of Coconut Grove, from the artistic energy of Midtown to the waterfront serenity of Edgewater—each area offers unique spaces to practice and connect.

Miami's year-round sunshine means we can practice outdoors whenever you'd like—rooftop sessions with sunrise views, bayfront flows with ocean breezes, or park practices surrounded by tropical greenery. Your practice can be as dynamic and vibrant as the city itself.

Most importantly, Miami's community has embraced wellness in such an authentic way. People here understand that taking care of yourself isn't selfish—it's essential. I'm honored to be part of this city's wellness landscape, bringing personalized yoga to the spaces where you live, work, and thrive.
