Quick Questions

How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, though last-minute sessions are sometimes available. For regular weekly slots, booking 1-2 weeks ahead ensures your preferred time.

What's your cancellation policy?

Please provide 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Life happens—if you need to cancel with less notice due to illness or emergency, just let me know.

Do you offer package deals?

Yes! I offer discounted packages for clients booking multiple sessions. We can discuss package options during your consultation or first session.

How long does it take to hear back?

I respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, usually much sooner. If you don't hear from me, please check your spam folder or reach out via phone.
