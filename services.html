Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to start?

Absolutely not! I work with complete beginners all the time. That's actually one of the benefits of private or small group instruction—we can start exactly where you are without any pressure or comparison to others.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, stretchy clothing that allows you to move freely. Think athletic wear, leggings, yoga pants, or shorts. You'll practice barefoot, so no special footwear needed.

How much space do I need?

Not much! A standard yoga mat is about 6 feet by 2 feet. If you can lie down comfortably and extend your arms without hitting anything, you have enough space.

Do I need to provide equipment?

Nope! I bring everything—mats, blocks, straps, bolsters, blankets. You just need to show up. If you have your own mat you love, feel free to use it!

What if I have an injury or physical limitation?

Please share this during our initial consultation! I'm trained to work with injuries, chronic pain, and physical limitations. We'll create a practice that supports your healing, not hinders it.

How often should I practice?

This depends on your goals and schedule. Many clients see me 1-2 times per week, while others prefer weekly or biweekly sessions. We'll discuss what makes sense for you.

Can I practice outdoors?

Yes! Weather permitting, we can practice at the beach, in parks, on rooftops, or any outdoor space you prefer. Miami's climate is perfect for outdoor yoga most of the year.

What's your cancellation policy?

I ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Life happens—if you need to cancel with less notice due to illness or emergency, just let me know and we'll work it out.
